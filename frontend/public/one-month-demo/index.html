What is this feature?

The "One Month at a Time" feature helps users understand the value of delaying Social Security benefits by breaking down the 8-year wait (age 62 to 70) into 96 achievable monthly micro-commitments.

Key Components:

  • Three-Bar Chart: Visual comparison of benefits at 62, current selection, and 70
  • Month/Year Controls: Easy increment/decrement by months or years
  • Benefit Calculations: Accurate SS calculations including FRA and DRCs
  • Output Display: Shows monthly gain and cumulative lifetime benefit
